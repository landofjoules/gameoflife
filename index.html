Conway's Game of Life

An easy place to explore, explain, and experiment, with this simple, complex system.

Keyboard Commands

  • K: Bring up this menu
  • Space: play/pause continuously
  • Enter: single step
  • C/Esc: clear the board
  • Z: random board
  • R: revert to last edited formation
  • W: toggle wrap edges
  • N: display adjacent live cell count
  • T: show/hide toolbar
